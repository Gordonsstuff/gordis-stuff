to create a world that has stopped moving

“I wish the world would stop spinning” That's what my grandfather said as he reflects on life and the passage of time. His words hit me like a truck and I take on this task. I look at architecture and the structures that hide the passage of time. In a windowless room, time is masked and the only factor of time are the inhabitants of the space. I take materials used in architecture; wood and concrete, and construct these forms that may outlive us all. In doing so I create a world that has stopped spinning.
