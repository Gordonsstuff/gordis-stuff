to hold onto the past

Memories are small markers in life, and if a memory is forgotten. That mark dissapears, to have a chance to resurface again. My grandmothers apartment has been renovate her intricate tiles replaced by light grey wooden panels that one might see in a modern home. Admitedly I myself dont fully remember what her old apartment looks like but the one thing that I do remember are those beautiful tiles that I walked on ever since I was a baby. From memory I try to recreate these tiles that I walked on.
